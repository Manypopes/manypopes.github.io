Desktop Three Axis Milling Machine
Replacement of machine control systems

Master's Degree Final Year Project

2016 – 2017

My Master's Project involved the complete replacement of control systems and spindle motor for a three-axis milling machine.

The machine required a replacement control board, stepper motor drivers, spindle motor and various other components.

The upgraded machine was tested through two milling jobs. Firstly a complex 3D surface was milled from epoxy tooling block, and secondly carbon fibre (CFRP) sheets were end-milled to produce body panels for a hexacopter.

The finalised replacement wiring for the machine, based around an Arduino Mega 2560 microcontroller, shown on the left are four new stepper motor drivers and a new spindle motor driver.
